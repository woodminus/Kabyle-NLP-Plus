adlis -a , d asenqed ɣef wayen yuran s teqbaylit seg useggas n 1946 armi 2014 , ama d ayen yeɛnan ungalen neɣ tullisin .
mebɛed mi d- yefka deg tazwara amezruy n tira n tmaziɣt s umata akked tmukrist n wullis , nasserdine ait ouali yudder -d sebbat i yeǧǧan tasekla n tmaziɣt ad tidir deg yiɛewwiqen , ladɣa , seg tama n tsertit akked lexṣas n tzemmar n tira ɣer kra n yimruya .
acu kan , xas ulamma yewwet -d s leqseḥ deg kra n tira ur nebni ɣef yilugan n tsekla meɛna yenna -d d akken llan kra ttarun akken igerrez u yis -sen i tezmer ad tenefli tira taqbaylit .
am wakken i d- yefka , diɣ , kra n yiwellihen i ilaqen i usnerni n tsekla taqbaylit .
tazrewt -a , tebḍa ɣef sin yiḥricen .
aḥric amezwaru yettmeslay -d ɣef ungal deg 6 n yixfawen , ma d wis sin yewwi -d ɣef tullis deg 3 yixfawen .
deg uḥric amezwaru , yenna -d d akken d belaid ait ali i d bab n wungal amezwaru ‘’lwali n wedrar’’ .
syin yudder -d imyura imenza rachid aliche d said sadi d wayen uran .
ikemmel deg useqed -is i yimyura i d- yusan umebɛed , am amar mezdad , salem zenia , aomar oulamara , lynda koudache , said iamrache , tahar ould amar akked boualem rabia .
deg uḥric wis sin , yessenqed tullisin i yuran : meziane ou mouh , idir ahmed zayed , meziane boulariah , kamel bouamara , lynda koudache , djamila mimeche , dihya lwiz , rabha aissou , djamel arezki , said chemakh , zimu akked abdelaziz hassani .
i usmekti , nasserdien ait ouali yesɛa duktura deg tsekla tafrensist n tesdawit paris 8 .
am wakken i d- yella deg lmendad deg udlis i d- yeffɣen deg useggas n 2013 ɣer tezrigin l’odysée , ‘’de la pédagogie de projet et de l’enseignement de la langue amazighe en kabylie .
’ , d ayen ara yefken afud ameqqran i yinelmaden n tesdawit akked wid akk tecɣeb tsekla taqbaylit .
